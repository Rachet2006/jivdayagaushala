Our Work

Animal Care

Cows, bulls, goats, sheep, buffaloes, and mares—rescued from abandonment or slaughter—live in a peaceful, shade-filled habitat.

Medical & Hospital

On-site treatment and care for injured or sick animals; routine checkups and emergency response.

Feeding & Nutrition

Daily dry fodder plus 1–2 truckloads of green fodder; lapsi prepared on auspicious days.

Green Drive

1,000+ shade-giving trees; goal of +100 trees every year; ~250 planted in 2024–25.

Infrastructure

15 animal wards, 3 dry-fodder halls, 2 borewells and a dharam-kanta; staff quarters and saint accommodation.
