About Shri Jivdaya Gaushala, Bhinmal

For over 80 years, Shri Jivdaya Gaushala has served as the first Gaushala of Jalore district—caring for helpless and rescued animals in Bhinmal, Rajasthan.

Campuses & Land

  • Mirpura Gaushala: ~500 bigha
  • Panch Kuwa Gaushala: ~180 bigha

Together, these campuses are home to ~2,150 animals including cows, bulls, goats, sheep, buffaloes, and mares.

Key facilities include 15 wards, an on-site animal hospital, 3 dry-fodder halls, 2 borewells and a dharam-kanta, staff quarters and saint accommodation, plus 1,000+ shade-giving trees with a goal of +100 trees every year (≈250 planted in 2024–25).
